About Fewa Cargo Services

Established in 2010, Fewa Cargo Services has been providing quality cargo services to the residents of Pokhara and surrounding areas. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern cargo collection and delivery system and staffed by experienced cargo professionals dedicated to delivering compassionate transportation on time.

We are committed to our mission of improving the collecting and delivery of the cargo we collect through customers doors in cargo delivery.
